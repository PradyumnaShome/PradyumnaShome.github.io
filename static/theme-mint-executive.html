About

I research the intersection of technology, design, and business strategy with a focus on digital transformation and innovation management. My work helps organizations navigate the complexities of technological change while maintaining human-centered values.

Before joining academia, I spent a decade in the technology industry leading product teams at both startups and established firms. This experience informs my research on how companies can successfully adopt emerging technologies while fostering inclusive and sustainable practices.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in learning by doing. My courses combine rigorous theoretical frameworks with hands-on projects where students work with real organizations to solve actual business challenges. This approach has resulted in several student-led ventures and consulting engagements that have created measurable impact.
