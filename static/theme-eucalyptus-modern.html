About

I am a data scientist and machine learning researcher focused on developing interpretable AI systems for healthcare and social good. My work bridges the gap between cutting-edge algorithms and real-world applications, ensuring that AI serves human needs responsibly and effectively.

My research group collaborates with hospitals, public health organizations, and community partners to create data-driven solutions for pressing challenges. We emphasize transparency, fairness, and accessibility in all our work, recognizing that technology must be both powerful and trustworthy to create lasting positive impact.

Approach to AI Ethics

I believe that ethical considerations must be embedded throughout the research process, not added as an afterthought. My lab actively works on explainable AI methods, fairness-aware algorithms, and participatory design approaches that include stakeholders from diverse backgrounds in shaping our research directions and outcomes.
